About 19:30PM

19:30PM is a solar outdoor lighting brand focused on clean-lined products that improve how residential spaces feel at night. Rather than chasing overly decorative forms, the brand emphasizes modern silhouettes, calm atmosphere, and lighting that supports the architecture of the home.

The solar bollard light is the current hero product because it expresses that idea clearly: simple structure, elegant repetition, and a strong relationship to pathways, borders, and home entry scenes.

The overall visual direction is warm, dark, and refined — inspired by front porches, illuminated stone paths, and the comfortable quiet of evening.
